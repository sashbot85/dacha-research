💡 Key Findings & Recommendations

🏆 Top Picks

  • CDPH (Jessie Yang) — $21,838/unit, 71.3sqm, kitchen included, 45-day production, 28-year-old company. $174,700 for 8 units.
  • Mars Cabin (Martin Zhang) — $30,137/unit FOB for 8 units, fully turnkey with kitchen/bath/plumbing/electrical. Fastest assembly: 3-5 days. Pre-assembles 1 unit for QC.
  • Wuhan Sijiayu (Shirely Jiang) — Fastest assembly quote (4 workers, 5-7 days). R-30 insulation for -30°C. Steel + wood frame options. Awaiting pricing.
  • March House (Lin Wayne) — $22,800/unit at $300/sqm. 900+ projects in 90+ countries. Supervisor available at $130/day.

💰 Pricing Insights

  • Price range: $263–$638/sqm across all quoted suppliers
  • Budget tier ($260-$300/sqm): Xiacheng, March House, CDPH — structure + basic finish
  • Mid tier ($300-$420/sqm): Sinova, Senfya, Sunfine, Shenghang — more included
  • Premium tier ($400-$640/sqm): Mars Cabin — fully turnkey with all fixtures
  • Shell-only kits available from $46-$90/sqm but require full local build-out
  • Volume discount confirmed: CDPH dropped from $183,200 → $174,700 for 8 units
  • Estimated shipping to Toronto/Hamilton: $2,500/container

🔧 Assembly & Logistics

  • Fastest: Mars Cabin — 4 workers, 3-5 days/unit (steel frame, pre-assembled sections)
  • Fast: Sijiayu — 4 workers, 5-7 days/unit (fastest competitive quote)
  • Standard: March House — 3-5 workers, 15-20 days/unit (wooden frame, more labor)
  • Equipment needed: crane + forklift (most suppliers)
  • Supervisor rates: $120-$130/day + flights/visa/meals
  • All suppliers provide assembly manuals/drawings
  • Mars Cabin offers pre-assembly of 1 unit for inspection before shipping

📋 Certification Notes

  • DeepBlue SmartHouse — ICC-ES ESR-5348 (US building code certified). Best for North American compliance.
  • Sinova — Claims CSA certified materials (unverified)
  • No suppliers have CSA A277 certification (not required for Ontario)
  • Most suppliers have CE, ISO certifications (European/Chinese standards)
  • Ontario building permits based on OBC — may need local engineer sign-off regardless of supplier certs
  • Insulation: R-30+ required for -30°C winters. Most suppliers can accommodate with polyurethane/PU spray foam.

⚠️ Red Flags & Risks

  • Xiacheng — Cheapest quote but NO website, no response since Feb 27. Unverified quality.
  • Sinova — No website, seems container-focused rather than A-frame specialist
  • Shell-only kits ($46-90/sqm) require significant local build-out — hidden costs
  • Sunfine — Honest that they haven't built larger than 65sqm. May not fit 76sqm spec.
  • EXW vs FOB pricing: Sunfine quotes EXW (buyer arranges transport from factory)
  • CDPH contact (Jessie) getting frustrated with repeated questions — keep follow-ups brief
