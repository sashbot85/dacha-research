💰 Price Range

Quoted prices range from $262–$638/sqm ($20K–$49K per unit). Best value at $300/sqm from March House and CDPH. Shell-only kits as low as $46/sqm but need full local build-out.

⚡ Assembly Speed

Fastest: Mars Cabin at 1-2 days (fully pre-assembled). Sijiayu: 5-7 days. March House: 15-20 days. All require 3-5 skilled workers minimum + crane/forklift.

🧊 Cold Climate

All top suppliers confirm -30°C capability. Mars Cabin offers 15cm PU + triple glass. Sijiayu offers R-30. Senfya recommends local polyurethane insulation applied after assembly.

🚢 Shipping

Most units ship 2 per 40HQ container (flat-pack). FOB from Qingdao is ideal. Estimated shipping to Toronto/Hamilton: $2,000–$3,000 per container.
